Text Adventure Game

(Created by yours truly and Youn Lei Eain)

Birthday Dinner Dash is an interactive text-adventure game that challenges players to think on their feet as they prepare a birthday dinner under time pressure. Developed using built-in browser functions like alert, confirm, and prompt, the game engages users with dynamic questions and immediate feedback, creating an immersive experience. The gameâ€™s logic, driven by if statements in JavaScript, tracks time lost based on the choices players make, leading to one of three possible outcomes. Whether you win by managing time wisely or lose by taking too long or choosing the wrong ingredients, this game offers a fun yet frantic challenge.
